About us

Tour-Ada.com is an an organization in collaboration with Ghana Tourism Authority to enhance and promote tourism activities in Ghana. it was found in March,2022. Our ultimate goal is to make tourism adventurous and exciting to ourntourists-both local and international. We provide major services such as whale-watching, crodile watching, Island-to-Island tour and kayaking. Other Activities include a trip to one of the most relaxing areas on the Volta River-the estuary. Activitiesinclude a motorboat ride on the Volta River, riverside lunch, and a river beach resort. Walk across the beach for greatviews of the estuary, interact with localsand visit a fisher foklks.

Island Tour

There are numerous sacred crocodile ponds scattered throughout Ghana, however one of the most famous ponds is on one of the islands at Ada,in the Grater Accra region in Ghana.Many ponds will have a fetish priest who can provide a blessing and entice a crocodile from the water with incantations and the protests of a live fowl.At most ponds the crocodile and chicken quickly disappear, but at Ada the crocodiles are much more habituated and will frequently pose for photographs after being satiated.

Kayaking

Read more...

Kayaking is a fun activity that involves moving through water in a small water vessel with the aid of a double-bladed paddle. It allows the boat driver to maneuver through waterways by sitting face-forward and propelling ahead with alternating side-to-side paddle strokes. The paddler sits in the cockpit with the legs extended beneath a closed deck, leaving the upper body free and exposed.The kayak has been around for centuries. It was first used by the Inuits in the Arctic regions for navigating waterways. It was a pragmatic way to hunt, fish, and transport passengers across the water. Its easy access and basic functionalities have made it the burgeoning recreational activity known around the world today.
